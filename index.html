How It Works

Your 7-step build journey

After login, a progress bar guides you through each step. Complete one, move to the next. Your personalized path is based on your questionnaire answers.

1

Getting Started

Personalized Questionnaire

Click "Start Your Journey" and log in. A short questionnaire builds your custom path. First-time builder? Walk every step. Experienced? Jump to what you need.

Free 4 quick questions Personalized
2

Financing

Get Pre-Approved

Connect with verified mortgage partners for pre-approvals — construction loans, traditional mortgages, and more. Multiple pre-approvals give you the strongest negotiating position.

Multiple lenders Construction loans
3

Land Acquisition

Find Your Dream Location

After pre-approval, contact Narrow to get matched with a qualified real estate agent in your area who specializes in land purchases and new construction lots.

EXP Realty Network Nationwide coverage
4

Design

Find Your Plans

Browse the architect plan marketplace. Each plan is listed by its architect — their business, their work, sold through Narrow. Search by size, style, bedrooms, and region.

Verified architects All home styles Secure purchase
5

Customization

Make It Your Home

After purchasing plans, work with the architect for modifications — layout changes, regional code adaptation, and permit-ready drawings. Turn a stock plan into YOUR home.

Real architects Code compliance Permit-ready

Construction

Bid Your Plans

Upload your finalized plans and request bids from at least 3 vetted contractors in your region. Compare ratings, experience, and pricing through the bid portal.

Verified & licensed Regional matching Rated & reviewed
